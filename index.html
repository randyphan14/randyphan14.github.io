About

I've always had a difficult time reconciling my enthusiasm for both the left and right brain in my career. As a full stack developer, my itch for logical problem solving, recognizing patterns, and maximizing efficiency are more than satisfied. While at the same time, I am able to engineer creative solutions, both visually and technically, giving my right brain the stimulation it so desires.

Meanwhile, my time in the service industry has given me the strong ability to work in and lead in team environments, stellar interpersonal communication skills, as well as an appreciation for efficiency. It is easy to understand why I have developed such a passion for my career - software development has been the perfect fusion of my most valued skills. As a person who craves challenges and intellectual stimulation, I so am grateful to have found this path and to be able to see where it leads me.
